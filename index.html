02
Product Design · 2025
Info Architecture Prototyping Research

K-Box

A personal knowledge manager that helps students stop saving things they never actually revisit.

The problem

Students save everything — articles, links, videos — but never go back to them. I mapped this "saving-revisiting gap" through primary and secondary research.

Key features

Flashback — resurfaces saved content at the right moment. Public & private resource spaces for solo learning and community sharing.

Design decisions

Built a structured information architecture: personalized categories, titles, descriptions, and favorites so retrieval feels natural, not like digging through a dump.
